About me

Designer who
ships impact.

I'm a Senior UI/UX Designer specialising in fintech, design systems, and AI-driven UX. For the past 3+ years at bonify (SCHUFA Group), I've led product design that's generated €400K+ in revenue and driven 70K+ subscribers.

My process is grounded in research-first thinking — I map user friction, design with data, and collaborate deeply with engineers, PMs, and business stakeholders to ensure designs survive contact with reality.

"I don't just design screens — I design outcomes. Every pixel has a purpose tied to a metric."

Recently I've expanded into AI-augmented design workflows, using Figma Make, Claude, ChatGPT, and Lovable to accelerate discovery, prototyping, and iteration. Based in Nürnberg, Germany — open to Berlin, remote, or relocation.
